About

English form sample of Voice Activated

Developper perspective

The purpose of this sample is to activate several forms with common fields. Side forms (left or right) can be show or hidden according to business rules (here a button).
Activation rules target specifically one field in one form, so we have three set of rules. After spoken, only the visible fields in the forms will be used.

User perspective

In this page, you have to talk in english. Infinitive verbs or second person. The red dot in the title means recognizer is processing, after a silence it restarts automatically. It has to be stopped manually. Password field is just a simple text to see the content.

On the right (left panel, right panel) of this page it looks like a register page, you can say :
  • fill name <<name(s)>>
  • fill password <<something>>
    -- lisible car c'est un text et pas un password
  • change remember me
    -- toggle the checkbox
  • connect me
  • change balloon
    -- show/hide balloons
  • change debug
    -- show/hide debug panel
  • change voice
    -- hide stop button BUT voice activation remains
  • stop voice
